What are we all about, you may ask?

ODBEEZ. An independent music production company founded by a team of five creatives. Based in Johannesburg, we specialise in recording, mixing, mastering, and custom music production — service tailored specifically for independent artists who need professional-quality sound at affordable rates. Our business formed out of a shared need we all experienced personally. As young creatives, we know how hard it can be to find studio spaces that are professional, welcoming, and financially accessible. As ODBEEZ, we aim to change that by creating a space that balances industry technical excellence with cultural relevance and community support.

Our target market consists of upcoming musicians, content creators, small labels, and youth-driven creatives across multiple genres, particularly hip-hop, R&B, Afrobeat and alternatives. ODBEEZ is positioned as a platform — one where sound quality and creative support, and long-term artistic development all come together. We’re not just offering studio services; we’re building relationships that help artists grow their craft from demo to final release.

What about our mission statement?

ODBEEZ aims to empower upcoming artists by providing affordable, high-quality audio production and engineering services. Our mission is to nurture creativity and professionalism in music through reliable, accessible, and industry-standard sound production, engineering and to nurture an environment where creativity thrives.
